Taper #7 : Wonders : Fall 2021
The Machine was grotesque by Esen Espinosa : << — >>

The machine was grotesque
as it was sublime


It was the silent sound
it made


It was the slow pace
of its movement


The feeling that my life will be over
before it finishes its path


The eclipsed light of the stars


Small lights dying
far away
behind the cold of metal golem parts


What lies in darkness
lies in shadows of a bigger dark


The machine was grotesque
as it was sublime
